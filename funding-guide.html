FSA Direct Operating Loans

USDA Farm Service Agency | Loan | Up to $400,000

Annual operating loans to cover seed, fertilizer, chemicals, fuel, and equipment. Beginning farmers receive priority consideration.

Key Benefits:

  • Low interest rate: 3.375%
  • Flexible repayment based on harvest schedule
  • Beginning farmer priority
  • Can refinance existing debt

Deadline: Year-round (apply before planting season)

EQIP - Environmental Quality Incentives Program

USDA NRCS | Cost-share Grant | Up to $450,000

Provides financial assistance for conservation practices including irrigation systems, soil health, and water management. Beginning farmers get 75% cost-share rate plus advance payments.

Key Benefits:

  • Covers 50-75% of conservation costs
  • Beginning farmers eligible for 75% rate
  • Advance payments up to 50% available
  • Technical assistance included

USDA FSA | Direct Payment | Varies by acreage

Key Benefits:

  • Direct payments - no repayment
  • Based on planted acres
  • Helps offset high input costs
  • Simple online enrollment
